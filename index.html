Levy Lab Research Project: Retrieval Augmented Generation for Pathology Reports

Tanya Nair, Eastlake High School

About: I had the opportunity to intern at Dartmouth Health's Levy Lab in the summer of 2024. As an intern, I worked with a team of three other high school students to apply our shared interest in computational linguistics to the Levy Lab's work in healthcare. Our project created a tool to help clinicians gain a better understanding of their patients' cancer cases by summarizing key details of relevant cancer cases from a large database of pathology reports. This tool uses an efficient artificial intelligence model to provide relevant insights without requiring extensive computing resources or internet access.
